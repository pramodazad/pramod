About Planisto

Planisto is one stop solution for all the rush and hustle you face while organising an event, either it be a family function, or a corporate event.

We brings all the people you need for your event at your doorstep by listing contact details of all related vendor which range from corporate event planner to Halwayi (Caterer) for your wedding

It is a free service, neither we charge you a penny nor take any commission from the vendor. All it does is to bring event organizing vendor and end user at a common platform.
